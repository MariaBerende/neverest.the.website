Marketing

Context of campaign and promotional activities

With our brand we want to change the daily routine of our target audience and inspire them to seek discomfort. Our social media campaign had to resonate our mission in a way to inspire our target audience to achieve the goal of seeking discomfort. We chose Instagram as our main platform as after conducting the problem interviews, we saw that our target audience uses mainly Instagram and Tik Tok. However, we decided to concentrate only on Instagram in order to provide better content. Our reach objective was to reach 1000 followers on Instagram. Our affect objective is to inspire our audience to change their lifestyle. Our Response objective is to have one of four people from our target group to like or comment on the posts that we share. The activities that we took to accomplish our campaign were creating different posts and stories that represent the message of breaking the routine and seeking discomfort. We even had our own photoshoot with a prototype of our hoodie, the end pictures of which we distributed on our Instagram page. 

 

Learning Points

Reach and awareness are easy to accomplish by following trends However, interaction and engagement are harder to do Posting regularly really benefited the brans as the audience can get to our values and aesthetics We need a detailed planning for each week to successfully reach our goal At first, we started without a plan, and we felt confused and overwhelmed with what should post and what is our goal. Afterward, we made a detailed schedule in Notion with each post and story and what is our goal. Each week someone was responsible for the posting and justification, and it was easier for us to keep track of the post. Original content (made by us) did better than re-posted content. Reminding people of a new post via Instagram story was an effective way of increasing traffic to our page Check Instagram insight more often to gain better knowledge of the progress of our Instagram page. 

Keep 

Posting more frequently. At least one post and one story per day to keep our audience engaged and at the same time to get to know the brand more. Moreover, keep the same time-schedule structure. Using Notion for detailed planning and content calendar. Using call to action on every post /story Using relevant hashtag. 

Change 

Be an active user, don’t just post / share content but also engage more with other users by commenting and liking their photos 

Future Planning

For the future of our brand, we plan to grow our social media presence by creating a TikTok account. For this purpose, we will be in the need of a more professional tool to schedule our content on multiple platforms, since Notion is limited. Moreover, we plan to have a budget and promote our posts to increase the engagement and as a result the followers. In conclusion, we didn’t reach the 1000 followers on our Instagram but we are positive that by continuing developing our social media platforms and creating TikTok account will help reach our goal in the future.
